Gone but not Forgotten - Ed Bishop

ArmadaCon III

We were lucky to get Ed as a guest in 1991 as he had a spot of difficulty finding
the Astor Hotel.

Besides being entertaining, Ed turned out to be a more giving guest
than would normally be expected. He sat in on another guest's theatre panel (Ed
was rehearsing in a play at the time and took some time out to be with us) to
comment on the theatre experience.

However, nobody realised how much of a
giving person he was until after he had to leave. It was announced at the Closing
Ceremony that Ed had donated his appearence fee to the Con's charity; Guide Dogs
for the Blind.

Ed Bishop sadly died in 2005.
