ArmadaCon 28

November 4th-6th 2016, Future Hotel, Plymouth.

There are twists and turns, and there are twists and turns.

For the first time, the program actually ran to schedule.

Well, the term 'ran to schedule' is used loosely, as the previously published schedule was thrown out and substituted for another at the last minute.

The two cameras functioned perfectly. So what was the cock-up?

Events elsewhere conspired against us. A number of the regulars attended a wedding, where another event (previously scheduled for another date), took place at the same time as Con 28. As a result, numbers were well down for this year.
