ArmadaCon VII

17th-19th November 1995. Astor Hotel, Plymouth.

ArmadaCon VII saw the official recording of the Con, using brought in cameras, for the final time. By now, affordable camcorders were available and events at this Con were recorded using a single VHS-C camcorder.

Because the camera was set to record at long play, the quality of the picture is lower than normal.

It is likely that at least one tape is missing as a tape is marked "2nd half".

Sadly, almost no photos are held by the archive.
