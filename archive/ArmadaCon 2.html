ArmadaCon II

10th-11th November 1990, Plymouth Arts Centre.

Over a year had passed before ArmadaCon II took place.

Even though the events during the day took place at the Arts Centre; the evening program happened elsewhere.


For the first time, there was a concerted effort to record the events. Two video cameras were brought in. I suspect that these were hired, as the master tapes were full sized VHS. Even then, full sized VHS cameras cost over 1000 pounds.

Photographs are also held in the archive.

Guests:

Adrian Cole (Author)
Roger & Linda Garland (Artists)
Brian Lumley (Author)
Anne MacCaffery (Author)
