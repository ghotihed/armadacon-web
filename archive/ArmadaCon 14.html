ArmadaCon 14

8th-10th November 2002. Copthorne Hotel, Plymouth.

With the hoo-doo of the unlucky 13 broken, it was decided to change the numbering of the conventions. Out went the years and in came standard numbers.


Something interesting happened this year.

The person who had scrounged photos for a Con website last year had now bought a video camera.

Together with the attendee who had donated video to the archive for the past 2 years; the archive now holds footage that's roughly on a par to the days when the convention was recorded officially.

A copy of the collated photos is also held by the archive; this numbers over 380 pictures.

Guests:

Mike Collins (Comic Artist)
Andy Lane (Author)
Dr Roy Lowry (Chemist)
Michael Sheard (Actor)
