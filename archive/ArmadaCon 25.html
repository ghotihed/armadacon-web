ArmadaCon 25

15th-17th November 2013. Future Inns, Plymouth.

Recording the Con had turned totally digital for the first time. Camera 1 was a spanky new camera, instead of a creeky old VHS. However (despite a better condition tripod being offered), the camera was mounted on a creeky old tripod. If you hear 'creeks' on the soundtrack, you'll know why.

ArmadaCon has a history of the program going 'pear shaped'. Normally, it's the first thing you throw out when you recieve the Con pack. This year, the program went more 'wobbly' than normal. As a result, the first few minutes of some events didn't get recorded as the camera operators didn't know what was going on. Having said that, chaos at Armadacon is perfectly normal.

With the aquisition of new software, files are available as Mpegs again. File sizes listed are for the Mpeg version.
