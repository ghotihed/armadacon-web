ArmadaCon 19

9th-11th November 2007. Novotel, Plymouth.

With the deaf camera replaced and promoted to camera No 1, all should've been well.

The demoted camera No 2 sulked; a fresh tape was inserted and then it died!

To add insult to injury, the tripod for camera No 1 went lame and couldn't be used any more. Most of the footage was shot with the No 1 camera on the No 2 tripod.

Last year's third camera was kindly donated for use as the new camera No 2.


This year saw an increase in the number of photos held by the archive. This was due to requests for another Image Archive by attendees. At this time, over 370 photos are held.


Guests:

Jasper Fforde (Author)
James Swallow (Author)
Jules Burt (Actor)
