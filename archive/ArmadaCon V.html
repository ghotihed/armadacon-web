ArmadaCon V

12th-14th November 1993. Astor Hotel, Plymouth.

For the first time, the 'official' duration of the convention was 3 days. Like many other conventions, attendees would arrive on the Friday evening, in order to be at the start of the Con Saturday morning. From this point onwards, events were programmed on the Friday for the 'early birds'.


Official filming of the convention was scaled down; only one full sized VHS camera was used.

Additional footage was recorded by an attendee with a camcorder. It is known that at least one tape is missing; on the camcorder footage during the Mike Jefferies panel, the full sized VHS camera is clearly seen being used.

Photos are very scarce; only 5 are held.

Guests:

Brian Froud (Artist/Illustrator)
Mike Jefferies (Artist)
Danny John Jules (Actor)
Brian Lumley (Author)
