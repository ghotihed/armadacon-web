Gone but not Forgotten - Dolorez Mantez

ArmadaCon IX

Best known for playing Lt Nina Barry in the Gerry Anderson series UFO, Dolorez Mantez was a delight to have as a guest in 1997.

She was happy to chat and appear in photographs with the attendees.

She even went as far as donating a unique UFO related item to the Charity Auction.

Dolorez passed away late November 2012.
