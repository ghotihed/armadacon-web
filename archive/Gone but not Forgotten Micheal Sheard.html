Gone but not Forgotten - Micheal Sheard

ArmadaCons 1999, 2000 & 14

Micheal seemed to have a limitless amount of energy. He acted more like an MC
than a guest, he loved to get involved in everything!

Normally, a guest would do a
panel or two and judge the Masquerade; not Micheal. What he did went above and
beyond what a guest normally would do.

He would hold the microphone for
attendees during other guests Q&A sessions, join in with the Turkey Readings and act
as referee for the games.

His legacy lives on with the cry of "Micheal's magic 13" at
the Charity Auction, as he liked to raise 13 pounds for every lot he help sell.

Sadly, he died of cancer in 2005; he will be missed.
