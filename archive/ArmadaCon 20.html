ArmadaCon 20

7th-9th November 2008. Royal Fleet Hotel, Plymouth.

A new location and a new set of problems. By far, the biggest headache was the lighting; there was a complete lack of it!

The amount of natural light was fairly poor; when the lights were switched on the No 1 camera struggled. The No 2 camera coped well, but that was only because it was more modern.

The number of photos taken was well down because of the poor light. The bar didn't have any natural light and that's where many of the photos tend to be taken.

Guests:

Jasper Fforde (Author)
Adrian Cole (Author)
Mike Collins (Comic Artist)
