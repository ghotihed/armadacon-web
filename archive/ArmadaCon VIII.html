ArmadaCon VIII

15th-17th November 1996. Astor Hotel, Plymouth.

ArmadaCon VIII saw the loss of video cameras.

It's unknown if anyone recorded anything using their camcorder. As you would expect, the archive doesn't have a single frame of footage.


The number of photos held is pretty low as well; at this time, only 27 are available.
