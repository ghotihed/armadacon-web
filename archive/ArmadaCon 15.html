ArmadaCon 15

31st October-2nd November 2003. Copthorne Hotel, Plymouth.

ArmadaCon 15 was originaly scheduled to take place from the 7th to the 9th of November, but it clashed with another convention.

So, it was decided to bring the date forward a week. Sods law being what it is, the other convention changed it's date as well.


Although most of the convention was recorded, a single full sized camera was used for most of it. The attendee who had donated footage over the past couple of years was an avid wargamer, so wasn't present for a large chunk of the weekend.

Once again, a copy of the collated photos is held in the archive.

Guests:

Rev Lionel Fanthorpe (Author/Presenter)
Andy Seacombe (Actor)
Richard Bonehill (Swordsman)
