ArmadaCon IV

7th-8th November 1992. Astor Hotel, Plymouth.

ArmadaCon IV saw the last time that there was a concerted effort by the organisers to record events at the Con. Again, two full sized VHS cameras were used.

Sadly, for reasons unknown, only one photo from this Con is held in the archive; despite a number of camera flashes being seen on the video tapes.

Guests:

John Carrigan (Stuntman)
Adrian Cole (Author)
Jaqueline Pierce (Actress)
