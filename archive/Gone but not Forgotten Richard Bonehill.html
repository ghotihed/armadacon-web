Gone but not Forgotten - Richard Bonehill

ArmadaCons 15 & 18

ArmadaCon had the privilege of being Richard's first ever Convention.

Besides regaling the audience with some of the funniest stories of his work in the film and TV industry (as well as what he did for a living before he got into that business); it was his demonstrations of sword fighting that captured the audiences imagination.

The remainder of the hotel was literally empty when these panels were held!

Richard sadly passed away in February 2015.
