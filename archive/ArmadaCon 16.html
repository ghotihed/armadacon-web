ArmadaCon 16

5th-7th November 2004. Copthorne Hotel, Plymouth.

ArmadaCon 16 was the last Con to be held at the Copthorne.

Sadly, the Copthorne had become too expensive; when you're a small event, every penny counts.


The two attendees that had been recording the convention for the past year or so, actually started working together as a team.

However, editing the donated footage is a problem as porting Hi-8 tapes to a computer is a little problematic; whereas VHS is simple.

Once again, a copy of the collated photos is held in the archive.

Guests:

Lionel Fanthorpe (Presenter/Author)
Bernard Pearson (Sculptor)
Stephen Briggs (Author)
