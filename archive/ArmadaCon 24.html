ArmadaCon 24

16th-18th November 2012. Future Inns, Plymouth.

Everything started out well; the lighting lessons from last year had been learned!

However, the smooth running ended shortly after the Opening Ceremony. Camera 1 died; as a result, video coverage is a little patchy this year.

Camera 2 was rapidly promoted to the No 1 position. Happily, Alan & Linda Marques donated their footage, so everything wasn't a complete washout.

For the first time, the video shifted from a 4:3 to a 16:9 aspect ratio.

Due to the editing software used, it's no longer possible to offer footage in the Mpeg format. Available formats are AVI and WMV. File sizes listed are for the AVI format (The WMV format is more compact).

Guests:

Dr Pat Harkin (Pathologist and SuperFan)

Ian Edginton (Comic Writer)

Marion Pitman (Author)
