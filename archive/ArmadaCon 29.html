ArmadaCon 2017

November 3rd-5th 2017, Future Hotel, Plymouth.

The naming format for Armadacon changed from the number of the event to the year. The last time the year was used, was to avoid the dreaded 'Armadacon 13'. This time out, the regulars felt old and wanted to freshen up the event.

This year, Armadacon was almost glitch free. The two cameras worked perfectly, but the lighting had its problems. Some of the bulbs had seen better days. As a result, some of the footage is a little dark. The event schedule almost ran on time. However, there was a discrepancy between the timings that the attendees and the organizers had.  Whoops!

Guests:

R.B Watkinson: Author

Chris Chapman: Television Producer, Director and Author

Jonathan Green: Author
