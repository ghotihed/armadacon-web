ArmadaCon 23

11th-13th November 2011. Future Inns, Plymouth.

You would think that the duo who filmed the events had gotten things down to a fine art. However, every time ArmadaCon was held at a new venue, a new set of problems occurred.

Camera 2 sulked by refusing to work properly with discs during the early events.

Camera 1 'died' when the power supply was knocked and the power switched off during a later event.

Camera positioning went 'pear shaped' at the closing ceremony when the opposite of the room was used to deliver the address. However, a guest saved the day when they donated their footage!

Another problem was the lighting; it took a chunk of the weekend to find out how the switches worked! As a result, some of the early footage is a bit dark.

Guests:

Alan and Linda Marques (Film and TV Visual Effects)

Nev Fountain (Author)

Jon Garrad (Author)
