ArmadaCon 1999

12th-14th November 1999. Copthorne Hotel, Plymouth.

This year saw a change to the numbering of the conventions. Gone were the roman numerals and the actual year was introduced.

The reasoning was simple; it was a 'nod' to the 1970's TV series Space:1999. Suffice to say, there was a heavy Space:1999 influence at this Con.


This is the fourth and final year where the archive is devoid of any video footage. It is known that footage exists, but it's a case of tracking the owner down and asking for it.

Over 100 photos are held in the archive.

This is also the first year where specific photos can be traced to the person who took them, as opposed to scanning the collection of collated pictures from persons unknown.
