ArmadaCon IX

21st-23rd November 1997. Astor Hotel, Plymouth.

This was the last ArmadaCon to be held at the Astor Hotel. It wasn't that we were fed up with the Astor, it was more that the Astor had changed hands and wanted to go in a different direction.


This was the second year that there was a lack of camcorders at the Con; as a result, no video footage is held.

The number of photos held is slightly better than last year, at 33.

Guests:

Martin Bower (Film/TV Model Maker)
Colin Greenland (Author)
David A Hardy (Astro Artist)
Dolorez Mantez (Actress)
