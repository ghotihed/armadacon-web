ArmadaCon 22

12th-14th November 2010. Copthorne Hotel, Plymouth.

ArmadaCon 22 was originally scheduled to take place at the Royal Fleet Hotel.

However, the hotel owners decided to sell the building to... ahhh... what some people would describe as the 'Tom Cruise Fan Club'.

Thus another venue was located and all room bookings transferred. Yes folks, we were back the Copthorne! The last time we were there was ArmadaCon 16.

There were a couple of camera related cock-ups; either camera operators went missing or a camera was facing completely in the wrong direction.

Happily, footage was donated by two other attendees; so all listed events are complete.

Guests:

Ben Morris (Illustrator)
Terry Molloy (Actor)
Martin Lavelle (Lecturer)
