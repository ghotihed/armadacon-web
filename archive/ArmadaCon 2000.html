ArmadaCon 2000

10th-12th November 2000. Copthorne Hotel, Plymouth.

At long last (after an absence of four years), the archive sees some video footage!

A single attendee was generous enough to donate a copy of the footage that they took with their camcorder.

It is known that others have footage, but tracing it will take time.

Until this footage is obtained, it's not possible to release a DVD of acceptable quality.


Over 100 photos are held in the archive.

This year also sees the rise of the digital camera.

The photos taken by them are small in size (low mega-pixels back then), but it's the quality that counts.

Guests:

Richard Freeman (Crypto Zoologist)
Brian Froud (Artist/Illustrator)
Roger & Linda Garland (Artists)
Dr Roy Lowry (Chemist)
Dave McIntee (Author)
Michael Sheard (Actor)
