ArmadaCon III

10th-11th November 1991. Astor Hotel, Plymouth.

The third ArmadaCon and the third venue. Mind you, ArmadaCon would reside at the Astor for a number of years before the venue changed again.

It's pretty much at this point when the format of the convention took root. In short, if something works, we'll keep it.


Once again, it appears that video cameras were brought in to record the events. The archive holds the full sized VHS master tapes for this convention.

There are a number of photographs held as well.

Guests:

Ed Bishop (Actor)
Adrian Cole (Author)
Terry Curtis (Sculptor)
Mark LaVille (Theatre Actor/Director)
Terry Prachett (Author)
