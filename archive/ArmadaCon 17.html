ArmadaCon 17

11th-13th November 2005. Novotel, Plymouth.

This year saw a new venue for ArmadaCon.

Being at a new venue brought a new set of problems, but that's only to be expected. Some problems were realised early on, but others didn't surface until later....


Our regular cameraman had bought a new camera and was using it for the first time.

However, for some reason he put the lens in upside down! He didn't realise this until Sunday morning. As you would expect, most of the footage from this camera is out of focus.

The second camera was no better off.

Due to the layout of the event room, camera 2 (being used for a wide shot) was pointing directly into the daylight. This played havoc with the cameras light balance!

The bottom line is that most of the footage from this Con is.... hmmmm.... useless. However, with some new hardware and software, the footage has now been salvaged.

This was the last time that there was large scale scrounging of photographs.

The guy that ran the Con website was retiring the following year and would no longer produce the Image Archive after his tenure.


Guests:

Rob Shearman (Author)
Dr Roy Lowry (Chemist)
Alan Lee (Artist)
