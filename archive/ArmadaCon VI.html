ArmadaCon VI

4th-6th November 1994. Astor Hotel, Plymouth.

Something interesting happened at ArmadaCon VI; the convention was recorded on an official basis again!

Not only that, attendees would take one of the cameras and record an event that wouldn't normally be recorded.

It is known that one tape is missing and another may possibly be AWOL; but a copy of everything recorded from at least one camera is present.


One snag is that some of the events on the tapes are out of sequence. If one tape had an event on it and there wasn't enough tape to record the following event, a new tape was used. The remaining blank tape on the first tape was used to record a shorter event.

This has made reconstructing the correct sequence of events at this Con very tricky!

The archive holds around 30 photos.

Guests:

Martin Bower (Film/TV Model Maker)
Alex Fox (Artist)
Mary Gentle (Author)
Talis Kimbely (Musician)
Dean Wayland (Fencer)
