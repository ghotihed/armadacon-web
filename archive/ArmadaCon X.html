ArmadaCon X

20th-22nd November 1998. Copthorne Hotel, Plymouth.

There was a first and last for ArmadaCon X.

It was the first Con to be held at the Copthorne, and the last time that a roman numeral would be used to denote the Con number.

Sadly, yet again, no video footage is available in the archive.

The number of photos held is well up from the previous years; it is estimated that over 100 photos are held.

Guests:

Martin Bower (Film/TV Model Maker)
Jim Burns (Artist)
Alex Fox (Artist)
Ken McCloud (Author)
