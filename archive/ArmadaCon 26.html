ArmadaCon 26

14th-16th November 2014. Future Inns, Plymouth.

Recording the events at this years Con suffered a massive 'wobble'.

It wasn't so much of a case that the camera operators not knowing what was going on. It was a case that they simply weren't available for a large chunk of the time.

The Fan Film that was held over from last year, was finally being filmed! As the two camera operators were involved with the production, a 'stand in' operator was used. As a result, some of the footage suffered from framing issues.

Even worse; Camera 1 sulked and refused to give up its footage in Hi-Def. It had to be pulled off in standard definition. As a result; the quality of the footage isn't as good as it should've been.

The number of photos held by the Archive was well down; possibly this was due to the production of the Fan Film.

Guests:

Andrew Cartmel: (Novelist, Screenwriter and Comic Writer)

Philip Reeve: (Author and Illustrator)
