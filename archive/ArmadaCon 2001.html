ArmadaCon 2001

9th-11th November 2001. Copthorne Hotel, Plymouth.

This was the last time that the year of the Con formed part of the title.

On one hand, it formed an obvious 'nod' to the film 2001: A Space Odyssey. On the other, it got around the fact that this was the 13th ArmadaCon.


Once again, an attendee kindly donated a copy of their footage to the archive. Sadly, the quality is such that a DVD release isn't possible until further footage is obtained.

This is the first year that saw a jump in the number of photos held by the archive.

One attendee made a concerted effort to collate photos from other attendees for website usage. The archive holds a copy of that collection; it numbers over 560 photos!

Guests:

Jonathan Downes (Crypto Zoologist)
Richard Freeman (Crypto Zoologist)
Dr Roy Lowry (Chemist)
Stephen Palmer (Author)
Dave McIntee (Author)
