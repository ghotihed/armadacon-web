ArmadaCon 18

10th-12th November 2006. Novotel, Plymouth.

With the problems encountered at Con 17 addressed; ArmadaCon 18 should've been a breeze. However, fate had put a spanner in the works.

The Novotel's heating system was 'off-line'. So that nobody was forced to wear their winter woolies, large scale heaters had been brought in by the hotel........


Having learnt his lesson about putting lenses in cameras the right way up, our cameraman fell foul of another foible from 'Camera 1'. It had the habit of winding back 30 seconds of tape before it would eject it.

Normally, this wouldn't have been a problem. However, the sound on 'Camera 2' had completely packed up even though a borrowed microphone had been plugged into it. As a result, some footage is silent.

A first for the archive is footage from a third camera.

With no Image Archive being produced, the number of photos held in the archive is well down.


Guests:

Paul Cornell (Author)
Trevor Truran (Games Designer)
Dr Roy Lowry (Chemist)
Richard Bonehill (Fight Arranger/Actor)
