Charity

In the over 20 years of ArmadaCon's existance, the Charity Auction and Turkey Reading events have raised money in support of blind related charities.

During the early years, the charity was Guide Dogs for the Blind; where a dog was bought and named Geordi (after the Star Trek : The Next Generation character Geordi LaForge) by the attendees of ArmadaCon.

Later, the charity was changed to Talking Books for the Blind.

At the time, it was felt that very few Sci-Fi and related books were available; so ArmadaCon raised the money to ensure that these types of books were recorded.
