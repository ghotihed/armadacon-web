ArmadaCon 27

November 13th-15th 2015, Future Hotel, Plymouth.

With the bulk of the Fan Film having been done. You'd think that some sort of 'normality' would return to the running of Armadacon.

Not a bit of it. The renown Armadacon 'wobble' returned in the shape of a 'cock-up on the catering front' at the Hotel. The Bar was drained of real ale on the Friday. The kitchen was short staffed on the Saturday.

This lead to delays in the running of the program.

Somehow. The Con did finish on time, but only just.

Camera 1 stopped sulking and actually gave up its Hi-Def footage this year. However. The tripod for camera 1 decided to break and had to be held together with insulation tape.

There was a glitch on camera 2. This was caused when the operator of camera 1 stuck the power lead into the wrong socket. This lead to the Opening Ceremony footage for this camera to be lost. Ooops!
