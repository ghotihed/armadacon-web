ArmadaCon I

September 1988. Ballard Centre, Plymouth

Sadly, the archive has nothing from this convention.

It's not surprising really; I expect that most people thought that ArmadaCon I would be like any other convention, disposable.

It is known that there weren't any video cameras present (nobody could afford them back then). As for photos; I suspect that there were a few pictures taken, but as of yet none have surfaced.
