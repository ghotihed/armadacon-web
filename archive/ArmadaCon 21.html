ArmadaCon 21

30th October-1st November 2009. Royal Fleet Hotel, Plymouth.

For this year, camera No 1 had been replaced with a high quality SVHS jobby to compliment the quality of the digital camera No 2.

The lighting problem in the main hall had also been fixed with extra ceiling lights.

All should've been fine and dandy; however........

Two of the guests wanted to hold their panels in a different part of the main hall than normal.

Although there was ample light, there wasn't any power sockets to enable the cameras to be placed in a location to make the best use of it.

As a result, a single camera had to be used where the guest was lit in the wrong direction.

For the second year running, there's no footage of the popular Turkey Readings; this was because of the poor lighting in the bar.

It was decided to hold the Closing Ceremony in the bar area; as expected, the footage is of low quality.

Guests:

Rev Lionel Fanthorpe (Author, Presenter)
Alan Marques (Visual Effects)
Bob Baker (TV-Film Writer)
